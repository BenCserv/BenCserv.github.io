    Recipe Instructions

  • 1.Begin to boil water and salt the water.

  • 2. Throw in the Tortellini noodles when pot reaches boiling point.

  • 3. Once the noodles are cooked, empty them into a strainer.

  • 4. Add the pasta back to the pot on low, throw in 1/3rd a stick of butter and season with garlic salt, and italian seasoning blend.

  • 5. Add olive oil to the noodles and stir, the seasonings will soak into the noodles.

  • 6. Before adding your sauce choice, throw in basil and stir it.

  • 7. Now, you may add the sauce and noodles together, layer it over with the shredded parmesan and crushed basil, to complete your Tortellini dish.
