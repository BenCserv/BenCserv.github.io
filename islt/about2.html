About

As the creator of Fresco Italiano - Be Your Own Chef, I'm sure you're interested in knowing my background and why cooking. I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago, going to school, playing sports, and being active. So when I was out of the house, the only thing that brought me back home was my mother's cooking. Always cooking large, Italian, family styled meals for the 5 people in my house was like watching a work of art being made.


As I began to grow up and live on my own, I soon started developing my own recipes and cooking cabinet full of great dishes. Now, I not only want to share my blends and tastes, but gather other peoples as well, so you can have the best Italian dishes to add to your recipe book!
